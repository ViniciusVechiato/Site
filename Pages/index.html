Extra Activities

Since September 2018, I´m a member of Rotaract Club São José do Rio Preto Pálacio das Águas, where we do a lot of volunteer work in order to help the community. We have some projects such as: We sell Gnocchi and Japanese food to raise money for our humanitary projects. The Biggest project that we have is called Novos Olhares (New Eyes), where we select kids for poor families and provide an ophthalmic exam for them totally free, in order to check if they need to wear glasses or contacts. On the last edition of this project, over 700 kids were examed. In this link, you can know more about the project Novos Olhares Project

Currently, at the Rotaract, I´m the Secretary of my club, responsable to help organise our meetings and keep the minutes of every meeting that we have.

In my university, on the second week of October we host an event named Semana da Computação(Computing Week), where the third year students are responsable to organise how the event will be.

This event brings some seminars and lectures from entreupeners and professional people on the Computer Science area to talk with the students about how is the market and what is being currently developed by the companies, creating this way a network between the computer students and the companies. Also, we invite some professors to teach courses of an area related to the professional.

In 2018, I was at the team that is responsable to organise the event and since I started the university, I have not miss one single Computing Week.

In a partnership with the Fundação Estudar, create by Jorge Paulo Lehmann, my Rotaract Club hosted one of the many courses provided by this foundation, the Liderança na Prática 16 hrs course. A course focused on lidership and planning for people from 18 to 30 years old. It was during the hole weekend and it´s a very practical course, where the professors teach the students using real cases and examples to make the undestanding more easier and applicable for every other case.

This course was focused on develop lidership strategies by understanding how big liders think and act on their companies. It also helps you develop your personal objectives and how to achieve them using a spring technique.

In February 2018, I went to the St Giles Central London, in London, England to improve my knowledge in the English Language and get my Proeficiency Exam (IELTS), which I got a C1 grade. That was my first time in a foreign country and one of the best experiences that I ever had. In St Giles, I took the advanced English classes which were more focused on conversation and advanced grammar.

During this time in England, I got the chance to meet people from different cultures, which make my experience much more enjoyable.
